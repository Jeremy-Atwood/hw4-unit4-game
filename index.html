Crystal Collector

Here's what you do!


  1. You will be given a random number to achieve at the start of the game titled: Goal
  2. There are four crystals
    • Blue
    • Green
    • Red
    • Yellow
  3. Clicking on the crystals will give you a random amount of points in your total score
  4. You win the game if you total score is equal to the goal.
  5. You will lose if your total score is greater than the goal.
  6. You will not know the value of each crystal until you click on them to find out.
  7. Every time a new game starts the value of each crystal and the goal will change.
